# SusieQ Kitchen — Design Guidelines

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep rose (#C0395A) and warm crimson (#B02040)
- **Accent**: Soft blush pink (#F2A0B0) for highlights and hover states
- **Background**: Near-white warm cream (#FDF6F0) or full deep rose for full-bleed hero
- **Text on dark**: White (#FFFFFF) or blush (#F2A0B0)
- **Text on light**: Deep rose (#C0395A) or near-black (#1A1A1A)

## Typography
- **Headline**: Large, bold serif or display sans — commanding presence, generous tracking
- **Body / Subhead**: Clean, readable sans-serif at moderate weight
- **CTA Button**: All-caps or sentence-case, medium weight, tight letter-spacing
- **Hierarchy**: Strong contrast between headline and supporting copy — let the name breathe

## Elevation & Depth
- Minimal shadows — rely on color contrast and whitespace instead
- No cards, no rounded-corner containers cluttering the hero
- Full-bleed background: deep rose or crimson fill edge-to-edge

## Components
- **Hero Section Only**: Single full-viewport section
  - Brand name ("SusieQ Kitchen") as the dominant typographic element
  - One-line tagline or descriptor beneath
  - Single CTA button (e.g., "Order Now", "Get in Touch", or "See the Menu")
  - Optional: subtle food image or texture as background, kept dark/overlaid to preserve legibility
- **No navigation bar** (or an ultra-minimal wordmark-only header)
- **No footer sprawl** — at most a single line of contact info or social links at the bottom of the hero

## Principles
- One section, zero distractions
- Every pixel earns its place
- Fast load: no heavy frameworks, lean asset delivery
- Mobile-first: the hero must be as impactful on a 375px screen as on a 1440px desktop
